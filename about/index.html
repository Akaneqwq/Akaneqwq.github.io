Amur Tiger Re-ID Model Sep 2019 - Jan 2020

  • Through the research of the Amur Tiger Re-ID models, We devise a detection-features-as-attention(DeFAt) module, which lays more attention on the tiger region in the image by extracting the information of FPN in object detection. It can effectively improve the Amur tiger re-identiﬁcation accuracy when keypoint annotations are not available.
  • Xinhua Cheng, Jianing Zhu, Nan Zhang, Qian Wang and Qijun Zhao*, “ DETECTION FEATURES AS ATTENTION (DEFAT): A KEYPOINT-FREE APPROACH TO AMUR TIGER RE-IDENTIFICATION” IEEE international conference on image processing (ICIP). IEEE, 2020.
